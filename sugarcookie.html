Perfect Sugar Cookies

Ingredients:

1 cup butter (room temperature)
- 1 cup granulated sugar
- 2 tsp vanilla extract
- 1 egg
- 3 cups flour
- 2 tsp baking powder

Directions:

1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees
2. Cream together butter and sugar by hand or in a mixer until combined
3. Scrape sides of bowl and add in vanilla and egg. Mix until combined
4. Add flour an baking powder to the bowl. Mix thoroughly until dough comes together. The dough should be a Playdoh texture. Add a little flour or water if needed to get the right consistency
5. Turn out the dough onto a floured surface and use a rolling pin to roll dough to a thickness of Â¼ inch (these cookies do not rise, and they will not get thinner)
6. Use cookie cutters to cut out your desired shapes
7. Transfer to a lined baking sheet
8. Bake at 350 for 6-8 minutes, until the centers are puffy and no longer shiny. Remove the cookies from the oven before they start to brown
9. Frost with your favorite icing and enjoy!
