Mama's Goulash

Ingredients:

- 1 tsp olive oil
- 1 cup onion, diced
- 1 green bell pepper, diced
- 2 lbs. ground beef, lean
- 3 tsp garlic, minced
- 2 x 15oz. cans tomato sauce
- 2 x 15oz. cans petite diced tomatoes
- 3 cups beef broth
- 3 tablespoons Worcestershire Sauce
- 2 tsp seasoned salt
- 2 tablespoons Italian seasoning
- 3 bay leaves
- 2 cups macaroni noodles, uncooked
- 1 cup cheddar cheese, shredded

Directions:

1. Add olive oil to large pot over medium-high heat. Add onion, bell pepper, and ground beef and cook until beef is no longer pink. Drain excess fat and return pot to stove top. Add in garlic and stir until fragrant (about 30 seconds).
2. Pour in the tomato sauce, diced tomatoes, beef broth, Worcestershire sauce, seasoned salt, Italian seasoning, bay leaves and dry macaroni noodles. Stir and bring mixture to a boil.
3. Reduce heat to a light boil/simmer and stir occasionally until the pasta is tender, about 20 minutes.
4. Remove the bay leaves and stir in the shredded cheddar cheese just before serving. Enjoy!
