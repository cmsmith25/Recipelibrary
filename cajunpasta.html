Cajun Pasta

Ingredients:

-Salt
- 12 oz. Angel hair pasta
- 1 tablespoon extra-virgin olive oil
- 1 lb. chicken sausage
- 2 tablespoons butter
- 3 cloves garlic, minced
- 1/3 cup sliced green onions
- 1 tablespoon Cajun seasoning (Slap Yaâ€™ Mama brand)
- 2 tsp. paprika
- 1/4 cup dry white wine (can also substitute 1/8 cup white wine vinegar + 1/8 cup of water)
- 1/2 cup heavy cream
- 1/2 cup freshly grated parmesan
- 1/3 cup shredded cheddar

Directions:

1. In a large pot of salted boiling water, cook angel hair pasta according to package
2. In a large skillet, heat oil. Add sausage and cook until seared (about 5 minutes), then push sausage to the side and add butter. Let melt then add garlic and cook until fragrant, 2 minutes. Add green onions and cook until soft and fragrant, 2 more minutes. Stir to combine with sausage.
3. Add Cajun seasoning and paprika and stir until combined. Add white wine (or vinegar mixture) and heavy cream. Let simmer until thickened about 5 minutes.
4. Add parmesan and cheddar and stir until melted and creamy, then add angel hair and toss until completely combined.
5. Garnish with more green onions and parmesan, and serve!
