Cheddar Party Mix

Ingredients:

- 3 cups square corn cereal i.e. Chex or Crispix
- 3 cups square wheat cereal
- 3 cups square rice cereal
- 3 cups Cheetos or similar
- 3 cups Bugles
- 2 cups small pretzel twists
- 2 cups small square cheese crackers
- 1 cup butter
- 3 Tbsp Worcestershire sauce
- 1 Tbsp seasoned salt
- 1 1/2 tsp garlic powder
- 1 1/2 tsp onion powder
- 1 tsp dark chili powder
- 1 tsp smoke paprika
- 1/4 tsp gound cumin

Directions:

1. Preheat the oven to 250Â°F. Directly on the pan or in an x-large mixing bowl, mix together cereals, Cheetos, Bugles, pretzels and peanuts until evenly distributed. Spread evenly on an x-large baking pan or 2 large baking pans.
2. In a microwave safe bowl, melt the butter with seasonings. Add hot sauce to your taste. Stir or whisk until seasonings have dissolved. Pour over party mix and gently stir.
3. Place into the oven and bake for 1 hour 15 minutes, stopping to stir every 15 minutes.
4. Spread on parchment paper to cool completely. Store in an airtight container at room temperature.
