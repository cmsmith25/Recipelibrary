Instant Pot Burgers

Ingredients:

- 4 4oz ground beef burger patties
- ½ cup water (goes to the bottom of the pot to create steam)
- Worchestershire Sauce
- Burger seasoning
- Foil or parchment paper

Directions:

1. Start by adding Worchestershire sauce to beef and preparing the burger patties. Season each patty to taste.
2. Individually wrap each one of them in foil or parchment paper.
3. Place the patties wrapped in foil or parchment paper on the trivet in the IP, add ½ cup of water to the bottom of the pot. Cover with the lid.
4. Set the valve in sealing position, then click on manual – high pressure and cook for 10 minutes.
5. When the Instant Pot beeps and is done with cooking, do a quick release. Carefully open the lid away from your face, and take out the burger patties. If you want to brown the meat, just cook under the broiler for 2-3 minutes. Assemble the burgers with your favorite toppings and serve!
