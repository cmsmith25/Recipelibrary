Homemade Icing

Ingredients:

- 3 cups powdered sugar
- 1/3 cup butter, softened
- 1 Â½ tsp vanilla
- 1 to 2 tablespoons milk

Directions:

1. In a medium bowl, mix powdered sugar and butter with a spoon or electric mixer on low
2. Stir in vanilla and 1 tablespoon of milk
3. Gradually stir in enough remaining milk to make frosting smooth and easy to spread (if frosting is too thick, stir in more milk, a few drops at a time. If it is too thin, add more powdered sugar)
