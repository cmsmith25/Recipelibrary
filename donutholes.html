Air Fryer Donut Holes

Ingredients:

- 16 ounces Refrigerated Biscuit Dough (jumbo flaky)
- Â½ cup Granulated Sugar
- 2 teaspoons Ground Cinnamon
- 1 tablespoon Melted Butter
- Cooking spray


Directions:

1. In a small bowl mix the sugar and cinnamon and set aside.
2. Spray the bottom of the air fryer with cooking oil of choice. Cut the biscuits into quarters and place onto air fryer basket in a single layer not touching.
3. Turn air fryer on and cook at 360 degrees for 4 minutes.
4. While the donuts are cooking, melt the butter in a small bowl.
5. Immediately after the donuts are done, coat them in the butter and then roll in the sugar mixture.
6. They are best when served warm.
