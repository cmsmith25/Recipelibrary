Garden Salsa

Ingredients:

- 3 cups fresh tomatoes , about 9 medium-sized tomatoes
- 1 cup green bell pepper, finely chopped
- 1/2 cup red onion, finely chopped
- 1 jalapeño (with seeds removed)
- 1/4 cup cilantro , finely chopped
- 2 Tablespoons lime juice , freshly squeezed
- 2 garlic cloves , minced
- 1 teaspoon ground cumin
- 1/2 teaspoon salt
- 1/2 teaspoon granulated sugar
- 1/2 teaspoon vinegar

Directions:

1. Quarter tomatoes and cut your onion and green pepper into strips. Dice jalapeño.
2. Place vegetables in large food processor. Add remaining ingredients and pulse until smooth (or chunky depending upon how you prefer your salsa).
3. Taste and add additional salt if needed.
4. Store in refrigerator.
