Hello, and welcome to My Recipe Library!

I designed this site to make it easier for those self-proclaimed "amateur chefs" to organize
their favorite recipes and to find new recipes. I love to cook and bake in my spare time, but
a lot of the recipe sites you find online have so much extra content on the page that has
nothing to do with the recipe. My hope is that my users will utilize this site to keep track
of their favorite dishes and desserts, and they will enjoy cooking without all the extra junk.
I'm so glad you're here!
